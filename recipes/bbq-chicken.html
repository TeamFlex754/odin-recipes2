BBQ Chicken

Description

This BBQ chicken recipe has been in my family for over 15 minutes, and will be sure to knock your socks off.

Total cook time here is approximately 13 hours over open flame.

Ingredients:

  • 5 lbs assorted chicken
  • 5 lbs bbq sauce
  • 5 lbs charcoal
  • 5 lbs rosemary (fresh sticks)

Steps:

  1. prepare the grill to 800 degrees
  2. rub bbq sauce all over chicken
  3. place chicken on grill evenly spaced
  4. cook until amazing
