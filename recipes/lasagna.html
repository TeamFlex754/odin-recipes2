Lasagna

Description

This lasagna dish has been in my family for over 7,000 years. Everything is hand-made, and sourced from local ingredients from my garden.

This meal will take about 8 minutes to make from start to finish, and only requires a microwave and butter knife.

Ingredients:

  • flour
  • eggs
  • sausage
  • 3 lbs of fresh cheese
  • tomatoes

Steps:

  1. boil the water in a pot
  2. cook the pasta until amazing
  3. make the sauce until amazing
  4. add all of the cheese
